Who We Are

Codecatalysts is an open-source collaborative community where ideas meet innovation. We're a team of 6 passionate developers who work together in a transparent, community-driven approach to building amazing projects.

Our model is simple: anyone can propose an idea, we discuss it as a team, list promising concepts, and collaboratively bring them to life. Every project is open-source, every contribution matters, and every member grows together.
